The question asks how many two-digit numbers Pere can write using different digits from the number 2019. The two-digit numbers must be formed from the digits of the set {10, 11, 12, ..., 98, 99}.  The digits available are 0, 1, 2 and 9. Since the number cannot be formed by the same digits, and it can't have a 0 in the tens position.

*   10
*   12
*   19
*   21
*   29
*   91
*   92

So Pere can write 6 numbers.

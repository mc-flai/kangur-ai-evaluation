    *   A = H - 4

    *   H = A + 5
    *   A + H = 19
    *   A + (A + 5) = 19
    *   2A + 5 = 19
    *   2A = 14
    *   A = 7
    *   H = 7 + 5 = 12
5.  **Verificar:**
    *   A = 7, H = 12
    *   7 + 12 = 19 (Correcte)

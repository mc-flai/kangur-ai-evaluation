Let the roots of the cubic equation $x^3 - 5x^2 + 5x - 1 = 0$ be $r_1, r_2, r_3$. We are given that one of the roots is $r_1 = 2 - \sqrt{3}$.
By Vieta's formulas, the sum of the roots is:
$r_1 + r_2 + r_3 = - \frac{-5}{1} = 5$
We know $r_1 = 2 - \sqrt{3}$, so we can write:
$2 - \sqrt{3} + r_2 + r_3 = 5$
$r_2 + r_3 = 5 - (2 - \sqrt{3})$
$r_2 + r_3 = 5 - 2 + \sqrt{3}$
$r_2 + r_3 = 3 + \sqrt{3}$

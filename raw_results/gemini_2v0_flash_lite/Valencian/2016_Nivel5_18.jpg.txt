   *   Unitats: 6 * (1 + 2 + 3 + 4) = 6 * 10 = 60
   *   Desenes: 6 * (10 + 20 + 30 + 40) = 6 * 100 = 600
   *   Centenes: 6 * (100 + 200 + 300 + 400) = 6 * 1000 = 6000
   *   Milers: 6 * (1000 + 2000 + 3000 + 4000) = 6 * 10000 = 60000

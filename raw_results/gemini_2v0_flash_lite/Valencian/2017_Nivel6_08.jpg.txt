Raonament:
1. Sabem que tan(x) + cot(x) = π.
2. Recordem que cot(x) = 1/tan(x).
3. Substituïm cot(x): tan(x) + 1/tan(x) = π.
4. Multipliquem tots els termes per tan(x): tan^2(x) + 1 = π * tan(x).
5. Rearranjem: tan^2(x) - π * tan(x) + 1 = 0.
6. Recordem la identitat sin(x) * cos(x) = tan(x) * cos^2(x).
7. Recordem que tan(x) = sin(x)/cos(x).
8. Calculem sin(x)*cos(x).
9. Si tan(x) + cot(x) = π, aleshores (sin(x)/cos(x)) + (cos(x)/sin(x)) = π.
10. Combinem termes: (sin^2(x) + cos^2(x))/(sin(x)cos(x)) = π.
11. Com sin^2(x) + cos^2(x) = 1, tenim 1/(sin(x)cos(x)) = π.
12. Això implica sin(x)cos(x) = 1/π.

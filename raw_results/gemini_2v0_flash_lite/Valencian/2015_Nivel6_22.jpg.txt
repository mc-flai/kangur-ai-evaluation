The problem states that $14_b \cdot 25_b = 416_b$. Let's convert these numbers to base 10.
$14_b = 1 \cdot b^1 + 4 \cdot b^0 = b + 4$
$25_b = 2 \cdot b^1 + 5 \cdot b^0 = 2b + 5$
$416_b = 4 \cdot b^2 + 1 \cdot b^1 + 6 \cdot b^0 = 4b^2 + b + 6$
The equation in base 10 becomes $(b+4)(2b+5) = 4b^2 + b + 6$. Expanding the left side, we get:
$2b^2 + 5b + 8b + 20 = 4b^2 + b + 6$
$2b^2 + 13b + 20 = 4b^2 + b + 6$
Rearranging the terms, we get a quadratic equation:
$0 = 2b^2 - 12b - 14$
Dividing by 2, we get:
$b^2 - 6b - 7 = 0$
Factoring the quadratic equation:
$(b - 7)(b + 1) = 0$
So, $b = 7$ or $b = -1$.  Since the base must be a positive integer greater than the largest digit in the expression, we must have $b>6$. Thus, b=7.

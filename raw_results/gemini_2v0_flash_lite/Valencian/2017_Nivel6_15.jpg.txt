Numerador: 1 * 5 * 25, 2 * 10 * 50, 3 * 15 * 75, ...

(2 * 10 * 50) / (2 * 4 * 8) = (2 * 2 * 5 * 5 * 2) / (2 * 4 * 8) = (2 * 5 * 5 * 2²) / (2 * 2 * 4)
(3 * 15 * 75) / (3 * 6 * 12) = (3 * 3 * 5 * 3 * 5 * 5) / (3 * 6 * 12) = (3 * 3 * 5 * 3 * 5²) / (3 * 6 * 12)

Razonamiento:
1. **Definir variables:** Let 'x' be the length of the shorter side and 'y' be the length of the longer side.
2. **Formular ecuaciones:**
   *  The perimeter of a rectangle is given by 2x + 2y = 104.
   *  The longer side is 6 cm less than three times the shorter side, so y = 3x - 6.
3. **Resolver el sistema de ecuaciones:**
   *  Substitute the second equation into the first: 2x + 2(3x - 6) = 104
   *  Simplify and solve for x: 2x + 6x - 12 = 104 => 8x = 116 => x = 14.5 cm
   *  Substitute the value of x back into the equation for y: y = 3(14.5) - 6 = 43.5 - 6 = 37.5 cm

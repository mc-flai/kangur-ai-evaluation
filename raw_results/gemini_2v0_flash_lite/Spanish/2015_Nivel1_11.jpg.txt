Razonamiento:
The question asks for the perimeter of the figure made of unit squares. The perimeter is the sum of the lengths of the sides of the outer boundary of the figure.
1. Count the sides of the figure:
   - Top row: 3 sides.
   - Middle row: 4 sides.
   - Bottom row: 6 sides.
2. Add the number of sides: 3+4+6 = 13
3. Therefore, the perimeter is 13.

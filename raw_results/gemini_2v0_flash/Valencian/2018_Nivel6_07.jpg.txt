g(5) = 5 - 1 = 4
s = 4 * 4 = 16

B) f(4) * g(4) = (4+1) * (4-1) = 5 * 3 = 15
C) f(7) + g(9) = (7+1) + (9-1) = 8 + 8 = 16
D) f(15) * g(2) = (15+1) * (2-1) = 16 * 1 = 16
E) f(7) * g(3) = (7+1) * (3-1) = 8 * 2 = 16

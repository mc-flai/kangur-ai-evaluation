Raonament: Estimo visualment l'àrea pintada en negre de cada quadrat.
A) sembla al voltant d'1/2.
B) sembla al voltant d'1/2.
C) sembla menys d'1/2.
D) sembla al voltant d'1/2.
E) és exactament 1/2.

Entre A, B, D i E, A i B semblen tenir més que D. Comparing A to B, A has more area. The best estimate among all choices is then A because it looks like it contains slightly over half the square. Comparing to E which has exactly half the area, A seems the most likely answer.

Resposta: A)

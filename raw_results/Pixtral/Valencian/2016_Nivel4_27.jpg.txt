**Pregunta:** Si \( U + V = 1 \) i \( U^2 + V^2 = 2 \), quant Ã©s \( U^4 + V^4 \)?

**Opcions:**
A) 4
B) 8
C) 1
D) 3
E) 3,5

**Razonament:**
1. Comencem amb les equacions donades:
   \[
   U + V = 1
   \]
   \[
   U^2 + V^2 = 2
   \]

2. Utilitzem la identitat \( (U + V)^2 = U^2 + 2UV + V^2 \). Substituint les equacions donades:
   \[
   (U + V)^2 = 1^2 = 1
   \]
   \[
   U^2 + 2UV + V^2 = 2
   \]

3. Substituint \( U^2 + V^2 = 2 \) en l'identitat:
   \[
   2 + 2UV = 1
   \]

4. Resolent per \( UV \):
   \[
   2UV = 1 - 2
   \]
   \[
   2UV = -1
   \]
   \[
   UV = -\frac{1}{2}
   \]

5. Ara, volem trobar \( U^4 + V^4 \). Utilitzem la identitat \( (U^2 + V^2)^2 = U^4 + V^4 + 2U^2V^2 \). Sabem que \( U^2 + V^2 = 2 \):
   \[
   (U^2 + V^2)^2 = 2^2 = 4
   \]
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 + 2U^2V^2 = 4
   \]

6. Sabem que \( U^2V^2 = (UV)^2 \):
   \[
   U^2V^2 = \left(-\frac{1}{2}\right)^2 = \frac{1}{4}
   \]

7. Substituint \( U^2V^2 \) en l'identitat:
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 + 2 \cdot \frac{1}{4} = 4
   \]
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 + \frac{1}{2} = 4
   \]

8. Resolent per \( U^4 + V^4 \):
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 = 4 - \frac{1}{2}
   \]
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 = \frac{8}{2} - \frac{1}{2}
   \]
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 = \frac{7}{2}
   \]
   \[
   U^4 + V^4 = 3.5
   \]

**Resposta:** E) 3,5

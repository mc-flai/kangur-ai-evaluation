   \[
   (x^2 + y^2 - 2x)^2 = (x^2 + y^2 - 2x)(x^2 + y^2 - 2x)

   \]
3. Simplifions davantage :
   \[
   = x^4 + y^4 + 4x^2y^2 - 4x^3 + 4x^2y - 4xy^2 + 4x^2 - 4x
   \]

   \[
   2(x^2 + y^2) = 2x^2 + 2y^2
   \]

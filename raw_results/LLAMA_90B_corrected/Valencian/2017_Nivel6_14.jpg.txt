NE = sqrt(a^2+b^2) (2)

sin(R)/sin(S) = AF / AC = AF / Aa = AF / b sin(R)/sin(S) = AB / b sin(R) =  AB / b * sin(S) sin(R) = AB / AA^2 + BB^2 = AB / NE^2 = AB / sqrt(a^2 + b^2)

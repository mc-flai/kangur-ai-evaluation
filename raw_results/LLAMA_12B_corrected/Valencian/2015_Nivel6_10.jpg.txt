Raonament: A bicycle's gear (N) is a function of two variables: the gear ratio (P/P) and the number of teeth (p) on the pedal crank and the wheel spindle. The equation relating these variables is N = P/p. A triple of gears for a pedal crank, a middle gear, and a wheel spindle is being used. When pedaling, Joaquim (àlies Purito) accelerates with this triple of gears. After changing the middle gear's number of teeth, its pitch variation is increased by 35%. The task is to determine the change in the number of teeth on the pedal crank that would also increase the pitch variation on the wheel spindle by 55% (option C). The pitch variation is related to the gear ratio and the number of teeth on the wheel spindle, but the exact relationship is not specified. El nombre de revolucions $N$ de la roda d'una bicicleta quan els pedals fan una volta és una funció del nombre de dents del plat, $P$, i del nombre de dents del pinjó, $p$. L'equacióève menciona és $N = P/p$, on $N$ és la velocitat del plafone de la bicicleta quan els pedals fan una volta. Això implica que una major variación en $N$ comporta una variación igual en $P$ o $p$ en el seu producte. Si augmentem el nombre de dents del plat en 35%, $P$ passa a ser 1,35$P$, i augmentem el nombre de dents del pinjó en 35%, $p$ esdevé 1,35$p$.
